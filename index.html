Bidimensional Landscaping

Kent is a landscaper. He also exists in two dimensions.

Move Kent around with, W A S and D, or the arrow keys. Place the next item in the queue with space.

Earn aesthetic points by placing items next to (North, South, East, or West) other items according to the following rules

People like forests, so +1 aesthetic for each tree next to each tree.

Paths need to connect to other paths to be useful. +1 aesthetic for each path connected to a path.

People like their houses to be pretty, +1 aesthetic for each tree next to a house. People also like being able to get to their house, so +1 aesthetic for each adjacent path.

People do not like other people, so -1 aesthetic for two adjacent houses.
